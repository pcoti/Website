About Me

I'm an Enterprise Risk Program Manager at Autodesk, where I lead cross-functional initiatives to identify, assess, and mitigate organizational risks. My work spans cybersecurity, operational resilience, and strategic risk management.

With a background combining emergency services, public safety planning, and cybersecurity expertise, I bring a unique perspective to enterprise risk management. I hold a Master's in Cybersecurity Risk Management from Georgetown University and have spent years on the front lines as an EMT and emergency management officer.

When I'm not working, you'll find me fostering dogs, contributing to Wikipedia, or exploring new ways to make complex information accessible to everyone.
